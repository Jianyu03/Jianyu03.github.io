Research

Our research goal is to enhance the autonomy and generalization of robotic systems in open environments. By leveraging cross-disciplinary knowledge such as deep learning, robotics, and control, our research interests lie in Embodied Robotics: Spatial-AI perception, Cognitive navigation, Cross-embodiment intelligence. We have developed a variety of robot systems that operate in structured/semi-structured/field environments.
