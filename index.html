Introduction

I am an undergraduate student at Beijing Institute of Technology, majoring in Microelectronics Science and Engineering. My research focuses on edge-cloud collaborative language models, MEMS sensor design, and robotic systems. I have conducted research at Tsinghua University and BIT, leading to innovative advancements in secure edge-cloud frameworks and high-precision flexible sensors.

With a strong foundation in Python, C/C++, Matlab, deep learning, and simulation tools like Ansys and SolidWorks, I have led projects that received national recognition and awards. I am dedicated to advancing technology through interdisciplinary research and practical applications.
